About

I work on distributed quantum computing, quantum networking, and reliability/security of AI systems. My current focus is quantum data center architectures, entanglement orchestration, and compiler/runtime co-design for fault-tolerant distributed execution. Previously, I completed my Ph.D. at UMass Amherst (ACQUIRE Lab, advised by Don Towsley).

I welcome emails from motivated students and collaborators. If you are interested, please reach out with a short note on your background and interests.
